What it Does

Aligns narrative + device data

Notes often describe clinical seizures and patient experiences; PMDS provides objective measures and parameter changes. We align both to build a structured patient trajectory across visits.

Flags measurable care gaps

The v1 gap focuses on persistent clinical seizures across two visits without evidence-aligned optimization attempts, while respecting documented exceptions.

Explains the flag

Each flag includes rationale, evidence anchors, and suggested action classes. Ambiguity is labeled explicitly for human review.

Inputs

Clinical Notes

Safety & Governance

Advisory-only

No autonomous actions. Human-in-the-loop review for every flag.

On-prem + traceable

Designed for HIPAA-compliant environments with clear audit trails.
